Spandan

"Spandan" is a Sanskrit word which means VIBRATIONS. In education, cultural activities always complement academics. In XIE each year, the two-day inter-collegiate fest SPANDAN draws crowds. It's a time for presenting exuberant talents in a fun ambience.

Transmission

Transmission is the annual inter-college Tech Festival at XIE. This year it was held on 4-5 Feb 2017. We had a huge participation from colleges all over Mumbai, attracted by events such as Xavier Challenge Track (robotics), Lazer Tag, Robo Wars and other exciting, unique events. Transmission is a major festival on the college tech-fest circuit in Mumbai, with several repeat entries year after year.
